About Us

KOSAL FARMS is a multi-cropping farm surrounded by wonderful conservation forest and natural lakes in Preah Vihear province in Cambodia. It is a home to variety of crops including cashew nuts, black pepper, pineapple, coconut, mango, coffee and cacao.The whole process of our product made by professionals. So besides the delicacy we guarantee the safety of products we deliver, and you can get the experience to see how we made it at Kosal Farms. We do not employ much industrialized machinery but human labor. Thus when you buy our products, you help create jobs for the local communities in Cambodia.
